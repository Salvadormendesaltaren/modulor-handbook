## 01. Group Identity

### Basic Information

- **Name:** Modulor Studios
- **Structure:** Holding / Group of companies
- **Headquarters:** Calle Hermanos Álvarez Quintero, 2, 28004 Madrid
- **Corporate website:** https://www.modulorstudios.com
- **Total Group:** 125 people (+ 7 in Modulor central)

### Vision

Redefine how the world's most ambitious organizations design, build and grow, leading creative and technological progress.

### Mission

We connect ideas, people and technology through specialized studios to build solutions that advance organizations. We believe the best work happens when strategy, creativity and technology are built together — not separately. We work hand-in-hand with organizations that are shaping tomorrow, turning innovation into product, brand and experience.

### Strategy

Build a consolidated holding that gives each boutique the tools, technology and scale normally only available to large consultancies, without sacrificing what makes us different: small teams with criteria, autonomy and craft. The result: real capacity to compete for enterprise projects and budgets that a single boutique cannot reach. The AI Native Agency.

### Relationship Model

From "connected, not coupled" to "cohesive and energetic." The group evolves from a light coordination model (designed for diversification) to a real cohesion model that allows us to tackle big bets together — without losing the identity and autonomy of each studio.

### Elevator Pitch

Modulor Studios is a group of boutiques specialized in design, technology, marketing and digital training. We offer comprehensive solutions from product design to software development, e-commerce and marketing.

### Modulor OS

Modulor OS is the group's operating system: the framework that combines technology, processes and automations so each boutique can grow efficiently without losing what makes it different.

**The 8 value pillars for studios:**

| # | What we ask | What you get |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Alignment with common tools | The best in your discipline |
| 2 | Adoption of unified processes | Data governance and common tools |
| 3 | Participation in group dynamics | Intra-group collaboration |
| 4 | Openness to internal AI transformation | Internal AI transformation |
| 5 | Contribution to shared culture | Unique and differentiating culture |
| 6 | Reporting and cadences | Shared operations |
| 7 | Active lead referral | Cross-sell with real impact |
| 8 | Delivery efficiencies | Operational efficiency and higher margins |

**What we're going to change:**
- Unify common tools (Workspace 2.0)
- Unify cross-boutique operational processes
- Align liturgies, incentives and drive shared culture

**What we cannot lose:**
- Identity per boutique
- Ability to innovate in service
- Own culture linked to discipline
- Ownership per boutique

### Tech (Technology and AI)

Modulor Tech is the initiative to structurally integrate AI into group operations.

**Achievements:**
- Clear and shared diagnosis: Problem is not technological, it's structural
- Identification of concrete quick wins
- Defined phased approach

**Difficulties:**
- Informal communication culture: If it doesn't enter through the official channel, the system can't improve it
- Lack of formal cross-boutique processes
- Automating without defined process amplifies chaos
- Data quality and standardization (CRM)
- Team capacity and focus

**Phased roadmap:**

| Phase | Timing | Focus | Measurement |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Phase 1: Foundations** | Q1 | Automation pilots: Deal registry (Pipedrive→Notion), freelance contracts (Tally→Doc), communication tickets | Setup time, manual errors eliminated |
| **Phase 2: Integration** | Q2 | Connect flows: Operations + Finance + Communications in unified pipeline (Won→Contract→Kickoff) | Deal-to-delivery time, adoption between teams |
| **Phase 3: AI Native** | Q3/Q4 | Intelligent systems: AI-consultable knowledge base, auto-generated commercial presentation agent | Output quality, system autonomy, time-to-insight |

## 07. Inter-Boutique Collaboration

### Evolution of Collaboration Paradigm

**AS-IS: Connected, not coupled.** Coordinated but independent boutiques. Each studio maintains its core, team and identity. Modulor connects but doesn't integrate. A paradigm designed for diversification — but that limits the group's capacity to jointly tackle large projects.

**TO-BE: Cohesive and energetic.** A united group with real capacity to tackle big bets that no single boutique could assume alone. Shared processes, tools and culture that allow agile mobilization of multidisciplinary talent — without losing the identity and ownership of each studio.

## 09. Shared Qualification Criteria

### Budget

- Projects >30k€ for mendesaltaren, Tailor Hub and FIK
- Projects >10k€ for SSTIL and Nocodehackers
- Monthly retainers >3k€

### Timing

- Projects with at least 6-8 weeks timeline
- No extreme urgencies without corresponding budget

### Decision Maker

- Direct access to C-level or budget owner
- Ability to make decisions without multiple approval layers

### Cultural Fit

- Companies that value quality over extreme speed
- Organizations open to strategic recommendations
- Clients who understand the value of design/technology
