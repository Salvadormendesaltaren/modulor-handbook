## 04. Services

### Commercial Model

- **NO fixed packages:** Adjust deliverables according to client fee
- **Process:** Close the number first, then adjust deliverables according to amount
- **Most common ticket:** EUR2k initial to start and grow month by month
- **Liquid squad:** Flexible team (Lead Marketing, Designer, Filmmaker, PM) that adapts resources according to budget

---

### SSTIL - Main Services

| Service | Typical Ticket | Duration | Description |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Creative Direction** | EUR800/month | Monthly retainer | Creative direction for campaigns and brands |
| **Social Media Management** | EUR2,000-9,000/month | Monthly retainer | Strategy, content, community. Min EUR2k (monitoring + small paid), up to EUR9k large clients |
| **Activations** | EUR1,000/month | Monthly retainer | Brand activations and experiences |
| **Content Creation** | EUR1,500/month | Monthly retainer | Video, photo, graphics, animation. 8-10 pieces/month (2-3 videos + 5-7 visuals) |
| **Digital Strategy** | EUR100/month | Monthly retainer | Integrated digital strategy |
| **Events** | To be defined | 4 weeks | Event production and management |
| **Branding** | EUR6,500 | 3-4 weeks | Brand identity (tactical). For premium, refer to mendesaltaren |

- Monthly content strategy

## 05. Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

### SSTIL - ICP

- **Size:** Established brands, corporations
- **Sectors:** Lifestyle, fashion, food, hospitality, corporate
- **Minimum budget:** >~EUR15k or retainers from EUR2k/month

### Minimum - ICP

- **Size:** Startups, scaleups
- **Sectors:** Tech, SaaS, Health, Energy, Finance, DTC
- **Minimum budget:** Websites from EUR17k, retainers from EUR1.2k/month

### Purchase Triggers

- Product launch
- Rebranding
- New market expansion
- Funding round
- Need for rapid digital presence
- Seasonal campaigns

### Fit Signals

**Green flags:**
- Brand with clear product/service
- Defined marketing budget
- Internal team to collaborate with

**Red flags:**
- No defined budget
- Expectations of "organic viral"
- No clear product/service
