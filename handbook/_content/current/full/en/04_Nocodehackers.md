- **What are we?** No-code/AI training academy + Development agency

  - Explorers of Lovable and Vibe Coding at the limit
  - Only ones doing Vibe Coding for real projects (gaining experience and prestige)
  - Largest no-code/VibeCode community in Spanish (+10,000 students)
  - Dual model: training + agency services
  - Success stories with large companies (Iberia, Santalucia)

## 04. Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

### Training - ICP

- **Profile:** Professionals, entrepreneurs, product teams
- **Motivation:** Learn to build products without programming
- **Geography:** LATAM + Spain (Spanish)

### Agency - ICP

- **Size:** Startups, scaleups, SMEs
- **Sectors:** Any that needs rapid digitalization
- **Typical use case:** MVP, automations, internal tools, apps
- **Minimum budget:** >10k EUR

### Purchase Triggers (Agency)

- Need to validate idea quickly
- Have manual processes that consume time
- Don't have development team
- Limited budget for custom development
- Need mobile app without enterprise complexity

### Fit Signals

**Green flags:**
- Clear and defined need
- Understand no-code limitations
- Realistic timeline
- Defined budget

**Red flags:**
- Expect complex enterprise app
- Don't have clear requirements
- Very low budget
- Unrealistic timeline

### When to Refer to Tailor Hub

- Project requires enterprise scalability
- Need complex custom development
- Very specific integrations with legacy systems
- Advanced AI/ML (custom models)

## 09. Risks and Mitigation

### Short-term Risks

- "No-code is dying" (transformation to Vibe Code)
- No project pipeline (all inbound)
- No one full-time in sales

### Mitigation

- **Real experience:** We are the only ones with real client projects
- **Community:** The largest in Spanish
- **Dual model:** Academy + Agency feed each other

### Competitors

| Competitor | Difference with NCH |
|---|---|
| **Platzi** | Similar message, but don't have the same level of technical depth |
| **Hero Camp** | Similar offering, more product-oriented, more expensive than VibeCoding NCH |
| **Built** | Only automations |
| **Centeia, Racks Academy** | Similar message but no real experience with real products |

**NCH competitive advantage:** Experience building real products for clients + ability to explain and teach.
