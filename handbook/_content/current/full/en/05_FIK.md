## 04. Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

### Company Profile

**Primary:** Fashion and major consumer brands with operational complexity:
- Multi-market, high SKU, seasonal peaks, retail footprint, POS needs
- Teams that need stability + roadmap execution (not one-off projects)

**Secondary:** Funded brands approaching scale requiring platform discipline

- **Sectors:** Fashion, Beauty, Lifestyle, DTC brands, Retail
- **Geography:** Spain, Europe (Italy expansion 2026)
- **Minimum e-commerce revenue:** >1M EUR
- **Minimum budget:** >30k EUR

### Decision-maker Profile

- **Typical role:** CEO, CMO, E-commerce Manager, Digital Director
- **Motivations:** Scale online sales, improve conversion, internationalize
- **Frustrations:** Limiting current platform, manual operations, lack of omnichannel

### Purchase Triggers

- D2C brand launch
- Migration from Magento/WooCommerce/PrestaShop
- Growth requiring Shopify Plus
- Store redesign/rebranding
- International expansion
- Retail channel opening (POS)

### Good Fit Signals

**Green flags:**
- Brand with clear identity
- E-commerce revenue >5M EUR (or clear projection)
- Adequate budget
- Understand Shopify Plus value

**Red flags:**
- Very limited budget
- "Free store" expectations
- No clarity on product/brand
- Want another platform
