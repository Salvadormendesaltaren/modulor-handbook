## 05. Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

### Company Profile

- **Size:** Corporations, scale-ups with enterprise needs
- **Sectors:** Finance, Retail, Energy, Tech, Industrial, Media
- **Geography:** Spain, Europe, International
- **Digital maturity:** Medium-high
- **Minimum budget:** >EUR30k per project

### Decision-maker Profile

- **Typical role:** CTO, VP Engineering, CEO (startups), IT Director
- **Motivations:** Modernize systems, integrate AI, scale technology
- **Frustrations:** Overwhelmed teams, technical debt, lack of AI expertise

### Purchase Triggers

- Need for new product development
- AI integration in operations
- Legacy system modernization
- Lack of capacity in internal team
- Technical audit pre-investment

### Good Fit Signals

**Green flags:**
- Project with real technical complexity
- Adequate budget (>EUR30k)
- Access to technical decision-makers
- They understand the value of senior team

**Red flags:**
- Looking for cheap "hands"
- Simple project that could be no-code
- Comparison with cheap offshore
- No clarity on requirements
