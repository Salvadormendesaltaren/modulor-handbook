# Handbook Modulor Studios

> The strategic knowledge base for the Modulor group — identity, operations, financials, and governance of the five boutiques.

## What this is

Modulor Studios is a group of five specialized boutiques: **mendesaltaren** (digital design), **Tailor Hub** (software development and AI), **SSTIL** (marketing and communication, includes Minimum), **Nocodehackers** (no-code solutions), and **FIK** (Shopify e-commerce). Each has its own positioning, but we share clients, synergies, and vision.

This Handbook is the group's source of truth: services, financials, teams, strategy, and referral matrices.

## What it's for

- **Governance and strategy.** Consolidated financials, objectives, and roadmap.
- **Cross-selling.** Referral matrices between boutiques.
- **AI context.** Downloadable as context for ChatGPT, Claude, and other tools. Also available via the integrated chat on this website.
- **Onboarding.** Complete context for new additions to the leadership team.
