## 02. mendesaltaren Labs · 226

> "Our future practice"

### What we are building

- **A renewed value proposition**
- **A client relationship model**
- **A studio blueprint**
- **A way of working** adapted to the new context

### Model 226 - How it works

A relationship model oriented towards business innovation, combining strategic vision, business architecture, and functioning as an experience pilot.

**Phase I — Strategy** (1 week to 1 quarter)
- Vision definition, opportunities and scenarios
- Business architecture and roadmap design

**Phase II — Implementation**
- Resources are assigned and the defined plan is executed
- A project director leads and coordinates
- Squads of 3–4 people working by objectives

### Lab 226 with Tailor Hub

Together with **Tailor Hub**, we design and activate innovation programs that connect strategic vision, business design, and technology:

- **Co-creation labs:** Align perspectives, identify opportunities, actionable roadmaps
- **Technical audits:** Architecture, data, and systems evaluation for AI-driven transformation
