## 06. Collaboration Between Boutiques

### Evolution of Collaboration Paradigm

**AS-IS: Connected, not coupled.** Coordinated but independent boutiques. Each studio maintains its core, team, and identity. Modulor connects but does not integrate. A paradigm designed for diversification — but which limits the group's ability to jointly tackle large projects.

**TO-BE: Cohesive and energetic.** A united group with real capacity to tackle big bets that no boutique could handle alone. Shared processes, tools, and culture that enable agile mobilization of multidisciplinary talent — without losing the identity and ownership of each studio.

## 07. Shared Qualification Criteria

### Good fit signals for Modulor

#### Budget

- Projects >30k EUR for premium boutiques (mendesaltaren, Tailor Hub, FIK)
- Projects >10k EUR for other boutiques
- Monthly retainers >3k EUR

#### Timing

- Projects with at least 6-8 weeks timeline
- No extreme urgencies without appropriate budget

#### Decision Maker

- Direct access to C-level or budget responsible
- Ability to make decisions without multiple approval layers

#### Cultural Fit

- Companies that value quality over extreme speed
- Organizations open to strategic recommendations
- Clients who understand the value of design/technology

### Red Flags

- Budget <10k EUR for complex projects
- No access to decision maker
- Extreme urgency without scope flexibility
- "Vendor" expectation instead of "partner"
- Don't value design/technology as investment
