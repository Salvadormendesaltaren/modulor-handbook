# Handbook Modulor Studios

> The strategic knowledge base of the Modulor group — identity, operations, financials, teams and governance of the five boutiques.

## What this is

Modulor Studios is a group of five specialized boutiques: **mendesaltaren** (digital design), **Tailor Hub** (software development and AI), **SSTIL** (marketing and communication, includes Minimum), **Nocodehackers** (no-code solutions) and **FIK** (Shopify e-commerce). Each has its own positioning, but we share clients, synergies and vision.

This Handbook documents everything you need to operate, sell and make decisions within the group: services, pricing, financials, teams, ideal client, strategy and derivation matrices between boutiques. It is the group's source of truth.

## What it's for

- **Governance and strategy.** Consolidated view of financials, objectives, KPIs and roadmap for each boutique and the group.
- **Cross-selling.** Each chapter has derivation matrices with concrete signals and qualification criteria.
- **Leadership alignment.** All partners and heads work with the same updated information.
- **Onboarding.** New additions to the management team have complete context from day one.
- **Internal due diligence.** Structured documentation with metrics, team, pricing and positioning.
- **Context for AI.** This is the most powerful use — keep reading.

## How to use it with AI

The Handbook is designed to function as context for your AI tools. There are two ways:

### Integrated chat

Use the chat button on this website. Ask directly about the Handbook content and receive answers based on the information from your tier. You don't need to download anything.

### Download as context

Download the `.md` files from the **Repository** section and upload them as context in your preferred AI tool (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, etc.).

**Examples of what you can do with the Handbook as context:**

- *"An e-commerce client wants to redesign their store. Which boutiques should participate, in what order and with what indicative pricing?"*
- *"Write me an email presenting mendesaltaren services to a product director of a fintech"*
- *"Compare the margins of the five boutiques and tell me which has the most improvement potential"*
- *"Prepare me a synergy analysis between Tailor Hub and mendesaltaren for the partners meeting"*
- *"An SSTIL client needs an app. Who do I refer them to, what ticket do we expect and what do I tell them?"*
- *"Give me the group's financial summary to prepare the investor meeting"*

The more context you give the AI, the better results you'll get. The Handbook as base + your specific briefing = truly useful answers.

## Sensitive information

This Handbook in Full version contains confidential information about the group, including billing and margins per boutique, financial objectives, detailed pricing, cost structure, M&A strategy and commercial pipeline.

**This information is strictly confidential and its distribution outside the leadership team is not authorized.** Check the cybersecurity recommendations in the Repository section for best practices in handling these files.
