# Handbook Modulor Studios

> The knowledge base of the Modulor group — everything you need to know about the boutiques, their services, and how we work together.

## What this is

Modulor Studios is a group of five specialized boutiques: **mendesaltaren** (digital design), **Tailor Hub** (software development and AI), **SSTIL** (marketing and communication, includes Minimum), **Nocodehackers** (no-code solutions), and **FIK** (Shopify e-commerce). Each has its own positioning, but we share clients, synergies, and vision.

This Handbook documents what each boutique does, who they target, what services they offer, and how to refer opportunities between us. It's the group's source of truth.

## What it's for

- **Understanding the ecosystem.** What each boutique does, how they differ, and when to recommend one or another to a client.
- **Cross-selling.** Each chapter has a referral matrix with concrete signals: "if the client needs X, refer to Y".
- **Onboarding.** If you've just joined the group, read it all. In 30 minutes you'll have complete context.
- **AI context.** This is the most powerful use — keep reading.

## How to use it with AI

The Handbook is designed to work as context for your AI tools. There are two ways:

### Integrated chat

Use the chat button on this website. Ask directly about the Handbook content and receive answers based on your tier information. You don't need to download anything.

### Download as context

Download the `.md` files from the **Repository** section and upload them as context in your preferred AI tool (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, etc.).

**Examples of what you can do with the Handbook as context:**

- *"An e-commerce client wants to redesign their store. Which boutiques should participate and in what order?"*
- *"Draft an email introducing mendesaltaren's services to a product director at a fintech"*
- *"What differentiates Tailor Hub from Nocodehackers? When do I recommend one or the other?"*
- *"Prepare a summary of the five boutiques for a meeting with a potential partner"*
- *"An SSTIL client needs an app. Who do I refer them to and what do I tell them?"*

The more context you give the AI, the better results you'll get. The Handbook as base + your specific briefing = truly useful answers.
